# Template: LinkHub (one-pager)
GOAL: one beautiful page: who you are + where to find you. The 30-minute build.
PAGES: Home only, 404
SECTIONS: avatar/name/one-liner, link buttons (per-link click styling), social
icons, optional email capture (ASK), footer.
FEATURES: links in one config file, OG image, optional simple click counts (ASK
— adds DB; default no).
SERVICES: none by default.
DESIGN: because it's tiny, be BOLD — striking background (gradient/texture),
one memorable type choice; this template may break design.md restraint rules.
ASK: name, one-liner, the links, vibe word.
