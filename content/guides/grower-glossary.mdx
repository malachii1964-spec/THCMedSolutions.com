Grow culture speaks its own language. Here's the working dictionary — skim it once, then come back whenever a forum post reads like alphabet soup.

## The plant itself

- **Cola** — a main flowering bud site; the "top" every training method multiplies.
- **Node** — where a branch/leaf meets the stem. Spacing between nodes signals light quality.
- **Fan leaves** — the big solar panels. **Sugar leaves** — the small frosted leaves inside buds.
- **Calyx** — the actual flower structure that stacks into buds. **Pistils** — the white hairs; they darken and curl as flower matures.
- **Trichomes** — the resin glands (the "frost") holding cannabinoids and terpenes. Their color (clear → cloudy → amber) is the harvest clock.
- **Cotyledons** — the first round seed leaves. They yellowing early is normal.
- **Taproot** — the first root from a germinated seed. Snap it and the seed is done.

## Genetics & seeds

- **Photoperiod (photo)** — flowers when darkness hits 12 h. **Autoflower (auto)** — flowers on an internal timer regardless of light schedule.
- **Feminized (fem)** — bred to produce ~99 % female plants. **Regular seeds** — 50/50 male/female.
- **Cultivar / strain** — a named genetic line. **Pheno(type)** — an individual expression of it; two seeds of one strain can grow differently.
- **Hermie (hermaphrodite)** — a stressed female growing male pollen sacs; can seed a whole tent.
- **Landrace** — an old, region-native strain. **Clone/cut** — a rooted cutting, genetically identical to its mother.

## Training & technique

- **LST (low-stress training)** — bending and tying branches flat to multiply tops without cutting.
- **Topping** — snipping the main tip so two colas grow instead of one. **FIM** — a sloppier top that can give 3–4.
- **SCROG** — a screen/net the canopy is woven through for a flat, even table. **SOG** — many small plants flipped early.
- **Defoliation** — strategic leaf removal for light and airflow. **Lollipopping** — stripping the shaded bottom third so energy goes topside.
- **Mainlining/manifolding** — structured topping into a symmetrical hub of equal colas.
- **Super cropping** — deliberately crushing/bending a stem softly to reset height (high-stress).

## Environment & gear

- **VPD (vapor pressure deficit)** — temp + humidity combined into one "how hard is the air pulling water from leaves" number.
- **RH** — relative humidity. **PPFD** — light intensity actually hitting leaves. **DLI** — total light received per day.
- **Quantum board / bar light** — modern LED formats. **Blurple** — old-style purple LEDs; usually a red flag.
- **Inline fan / carbon filter ("scrubber")** — exhaust and smell control. **Lung room** — the room your tent breathes from.
- **Runoff** — water that drains from the pot; its pH/EC hints at root-zone conditions.
- **EC / PPM** — measurements of nutrient concentration in water.

## Media & feeding

- **Medium** — what roots live in: soil, coco coir, rockwool, or water (hydro/DWC).
- **Living soil / no-till** — soil built to feed plants through microbial life, often water-only.
- **Super soil** — heavily amended soil "cooked" in advance (see the Soil Lab).
- **Amendments** — dry organic inputs: worm castings, kelp, alfalfa, bone/fish/crab meal, guano, rock dusts.
- **Top-dress** — sprinkling amendments on the soil surface mid-grow. **Tea** — aerated microbe/nutrient brew.
- **Cal-Mag** — calcium+magnesium supplement; the usual fix for LED-era rusty spots.
- **N-P-K** — nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium: the three numbers on every fertilizer.
- **Lockout** — nutrients present but chemically unavailable (usually pH). **Flush** — plain-water reset of a salted-up medium.
- **Mycorrhizae (Mykos)** — root-partner fungi that extend nutrient reach.

## Harvest & after

- **Flip** — switching photos to 12/12 to start flower. **The stretch** — the height explosion in flower weeks 1–3.
- **Fade** — the healthy late-flower yellowing as the plant spends its reserves.
- **Wet/dry trim** — trimming at chop vs. after drying.
- **Cure** — the slow jar-finish that turns "decent" into "excellent." **Burp** — opening jars briefly during cure.
- **62 % pack** — two-way humidity packs for jars. **Loupe** — the magnifier for trichome checks.
- **Larf / popcorn** — small airy under-canopy buds. **Bud rot (botrytis)** — mold inside dense colas; the late-flower nightmare.
