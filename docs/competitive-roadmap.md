# Lake Erie Cannabis — Competitive Gap Analysis & Content Roadmap

_Research date: 2026-07-16. Goal: be better in every way than the top cannabis
sites, grower-first._

## The landscape (who we're beating)

| Site | ~Traffic | Strength | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Leafly** | ~5M organic/mo | Huge strain DB, dispensary finder, news, learn hub | Marketplace/media |
| **Weedmaps** | ~2.3M/mo | Dispensary & product marketplace, deals | Marketplace |

Note: Leafly/Weedmaps lost ~46% of organic traffic in 2 years — the door is
open for a better grower-first education brand.

## Where we ALREADY win
- **Grow Like the Greats** — nobody structures legend-based learning like this.
- **Build My Grow** — a personalized *plan*, more actionable than ILGM's strain quiz.
- **AI Plant Doctor** (streaming chat) — only ILGM has an AI assistant; GWE doesn't.
- **Bidirectional strain↔guide cross-linking** — unique.
- **Premium design + brand** — most cannabis sites are cluttered; ours is cinematic.
- **Hyper-local WNY** + a real medical provider.

## The gaps (what they have that we don't — yet)

### Ring A — highest leverage (cheap, mostly pure-logic tools + verifiable content)
1. **Visual Plant Doctor** — clickable symptom → diagnosis library (yellow leaves →
   causes/fixes/guide links). This is GWE's #1 traffic driver. Pairs with our AI chat.
2. **Grow Tools suite** — VPD calculator, DLI/PPFD calculator, yield estimator,
   feeding-schedule builder. Pure functions, testable, on-brand ("Tools" in the spec).
3. **Strain Finder quiz** — "find your strain" by effect/vibe (complements Build My
   Grow, which is about the setup). Mirrors ILGM's finder.
4. **Strains → 100+** — close the scale gap with curated, verified entries.

### Ring B — content depth
5. **Terpene hub** — dedicated terpene education (RQS covers this heavily).
6. **Recipes / edibles hub** — expand our one edibles guide into a collection.
7. **More guides** — autoflower deep-dive, IPM/pest management, environment/CO2,
   drying/curing science, common-mistakes expansions.
8. **Genetics/breeder + where-to-buy** — breeder profiles + affiliate seed links
   (Fast Buds, etc. per the spec).

### Ring C — community & freshness (bigger builds)
9. **Grow Journals/Diaries** — members log grows with photos (needs DB + uploads).
10. **News/blog** — freshness signal for SEO + culture/lifestyle.

## Marketing (owner-executed; I prep the assets)
- Launch kit: social bios, launch-post copy, hashtags, share images (OG already wired).
- Meta/OG/Twitter cards already in place.

## Recommended build order
Ring A #1 (Visual Plant Doctor) + #2 (Grow Tools) first — biggest SEO + utility
per hour, and both are mostly pure logic we can build and verify. Then Strain
Finder + strain scale-up, then Ring B content depth.
